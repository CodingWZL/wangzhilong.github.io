Biography

My research interests are mainly in material genomics and material informatics. At present, I combine computer science (machine learning, deep learning, data mining) with materials science (high-throughput first-principles calculations) to realize the rapid discovery and design of materials and shorten the research and development cycle of materials. The target material systems include energy materials (electrode, solid electrolyte, photovoltaic thin-film), environmental materials (adsorbent), biological macromolecular materials (protein), etc. I have published over 10 SCI papers in high-impact journals as the first/co-first author, with the total > 400 citations and H-index of 13. I was invited to be a reviewer of Applied Surface Science (IF = 7.4), and I wrote a Editorial for the special issue for Next Materials.
