Biography

My research interests are mainly in material genomics and material informatics. At present, I combine computer science (machine learning, deep learning, data mining) with materials science (high-throughput first-principles calculations) to realize the rapid discovery and design of materials and shorten the research and development cycle of materials. The target material systems include energy materials (electrode, solid electrolyte, photovoltaic thin-film), environmental materials (adsorbent), biological macromolecular materials (protein), etc. I have published over 10 SCI papers in high-impact journals as the first/co-first author, with the total > 800 citations and an h-index of 18.

Publications as the first/co-first author: Advanced Materials (1), Advanced Energy Materials (1), npj Computational Materials (3), ACS Nano (2), Energy Storage Materials (5), Nano Energy (3), ACS Materials Letters (1), Journal of Physical Chemistry Letters (1), Small (1), SmartMat (1), Next Materials (1), Journal of Energy Chemistry (2), ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces (2), Chemical Engineering Journal (1), Chinese Chemical Letters (1), Computational Materials Science (1), Cell Reports Physical Science (1), Journal of Physical Chemistry B (1).
